Mocha-Coco Smoothie

Description


        Prep Time: 5 mins      | Additional Time: 3hr 55 mins 
        Total Time: 4 hr       | Servings: 2

Ingredients

  • 3 cups brewed coffee, chilled
  • 1 large frozen banana
  • Â½ cup unsweetened coconut or almond creamer
  • 2 tablespoons coconut oil
  • 2 tablespoons cocoa powder
  • 2 (1 gram) packets Stevia In The Raw

Directions

  1. Fill an ice cube tray with coffee and freeze 4 hours or until completely frozen. Reserve remaining chilled coffee.
  2. In a blender, place 1 cup coffee ice cubes along with 3/4 cup chilled coffee, banana, creamer, coconut oil, cocoa powder, and Stevia In The Raw. Blend until smooth.
